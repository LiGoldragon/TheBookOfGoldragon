## The Book of Sol

*The Book of Goldragon* represents a different approach to the themes explored in [The Book of Sol](https://github.com/LiGoldragon/TheBookOfSol). While *The Book of Sol* focuses on the foundational principles of the solar matrix and the physical requirements for vitality, *The Book of Goldragon* delves into the philosophical and spiritual implications of these truths. 

Selected articles and insights from *The Book of Sol* are being migrated here to provide a more cohesive and refined perspective on Li Goldragon's philosophy.

Key themes shared with *The Book of Sol*:
- The 108 Solar Divisions and 360 Phases of Sol.
- The critique of modern dietary habits (e.g., Sodium Chloride Toxicity, Grains, and the importance of Ghee).
- The transition from the Age of Saturation to a state of Sidereal clarity.
